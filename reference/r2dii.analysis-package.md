# r2dii.analysis: Measure Climate Scenario Alignment of Corporate Loans

These tools help you to assess if a corporate lending portfolio aligns
with climate goals. They summarize key climate indicators attributed to
the portfolio (e.g. production, emission factors), and calculate
alignment targets based on climate scenarios. They implement in R the
last step of the free software 'PACTA' (Paris Agreement Capital
Transition Assessment; <https://www.transitionmonitor.com/>). Financial
institutions use 'PACTA' to study how their capital allocation decisions
align with climate change mitigation goals.
